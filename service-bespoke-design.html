My Process

From Brief to Launch — A Proven Methodology.

Every project follows a structured, transparent process. No surprises, no scope creep, no missed deadlines.

01

Discovery & Scoping

I begin by understanding your business, your audience, and your commercial goals. A detailed scoping session ensures we align on objectives, timelines, and technical requirements before any design work begins.

02

UX & Wireframing

Wireframes map out the user journey before a single aesthetic decision is made. This stage defines navigation, content hierarchy, and conversion pathways — the structural foundation everything else is built upon.

03

Visual Design

Brand-aligned design concepts developed in collaboration with you. Typography, colour, spacing, and component design refined through a structured feedback and approval process.

04

Development

Approved designs are translated into clean, semantic code. Development is staged — you have visibility at every phase via a private staging environment before anything is pushed live.

05

Testing & QA

Cross-browser, cross-device, and performance testing. Accessibility checks against WCAG 2.1 AA standards. Load testing where required. Nothing ships until it passes every check.

06

Launch & Handover

Managed go-live with zero-downtime deployment. Full handover documentation, CMS training for your team, and post-launch monitoring to ensure everything performs as expected from day one.
