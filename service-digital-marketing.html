What's Included

Every Lever Pulled for Maximum Performance.

Digital marketing that works is not about doing one thing well — it is about aligning SEO, analytics, content, and conversion strategy into a coherent programme that drives measurable commercial outcomes.

Technical SEO Audit & Strategy

A comprehensive audit of your site's technical health — crawlability, indexation, site speed, structured data, internal linking, and Core Web Vitals — followed by a prioritised action plan.

Google Analytics 4 & Search Console

Full GA4 implementation, property configuration, and goal tracking setup. Search Console integration to monitor keyword performance, impressions, and crawl errors in real time.

Keyword Research & On-Page Optimisation

Identifying the search terms your target audience actually uses, then optimising page titles, meta descriptions, headings, and content to capture that traffic and convert it.

Conversion Rate Optimisation (CRO)

Structured CRO programmes that identify friction in your user journey and systematically eliminate it — through heatmap analysis, session recording, user research, and hypothesis-driven testing.

A/B Testing Programmes

Scientifically structured split tests on landing pages, CTAs, forms, and key conversion elements. Every decision backed by statistical significance, not intuition.

Reporting & Performance Dashboards

Clear, jargon-free reporting that shows what is working, what is not, and what we are doing about it. Executive-ready dashboards for stakeholder presentations.

My Process

A Structured Programme, Not a One-Off Fix.

Sustainable digital growth requires a continuous improvement cycle. I embed that discipline into every engagement from day one.

01

Audit & Benchmarking

A thorough review of your current analytics configuration, search visibility, site performance, and conversion funnel. This establishes the baseline against which all future improvements are measured.

02

Strategy & Prioritisation

A ranked action plan — quick wins alongside longer-term strategic initiatives — aligned to your commercial objectives. No busy work; every action is tied to a measurable outcome.

03

Implementation

Technical fixes, on-page optimisation, analytics configuration, and CRO setup deployed to a staging environment first, then live — with full change documentation throughout.

04

Test & Experiment

A/B tests running continuously against defined hypotheses. Each test generates learnings that inform the next — building a compounding advantage over time.

05

Analyse & Iterate

Regular performance reviews against KPIs, with transparent reporting and clear next steps. The programme evolves as your business grows and the data reveals new opportunities.

06

Stakeholder Reporting

I present findings directly to senior stakeholders in plain language, clearly linking digital performance to commercial outcomes — no technical jargon, no ambiguity.
