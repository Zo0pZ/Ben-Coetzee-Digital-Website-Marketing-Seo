Why It Matters

The Cost of Getting a Migration Wrong.

Platform migrations are one of the highest-risk events in a website's lifecycle. Without experienced oversight, the consequences can be severe and lasting.

SEO Rankings Lost

Broken redirects and missing canonical tags can cause Google to de-index pages — wiping years of organic growth in days.

Data Loss

Poorly managed content migration can result in lost pages, broken media, or corrupted data — often impossible to fully recover.

Extended Downtime

Unplanned downtime during a migration damages user trust, conversion rates, and can have contractual implications for enterprise clients.

With My Oversight

A fully planned migration with pre-flight checklists, staging validation, and a clear rollback strategy — so your go-live is a controlled, confident event.

My Process

Six Stages. No Surprises.

Every migration I lead follows a structured methodology proven across national organisations and regulated industries.

01

Discovery & Inventory

Complete content audit, URL mapping, analytics baseline, integration documentation, and risk identification. The foundation that everything else is built upon.

02

Migration Plan & Timeline

A detailed project plan covering all workstreams, responsibilities, dependencies, and go/no-go criteria. Shared with all stakeholders before any work begins.

03

Staging Build & Configuration

New platform built and configured in a secure staging environment. Content migrated, redirects mapped, integrations connected and tested.

04

UAT & Sign-Off

Structured user acceptance testing with your team. Punch list management, issue resolution, and formal sign-off before any production changes are made.

05

Managed Go-Live

Carefully sequenced deployment during low-traffic hours. DNS management, SSL verification, redirect validation, and immediate post-launch checks.

06

Post-Launch Support

Dedicated monitoring period post-launch. SEO performance tracking, crawl error resolution, analytics validation, and rapid response to any emerging issues.
